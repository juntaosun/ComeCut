What is ComeCut? ​

ComeCut is a free, AI-driven cross-platform video editing tool. Our vision is to fully leverage the power of the open-source community to build a truly free, open, and extensible AI video editing ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Core Advantages ​

  • ✅ Completely Free: No usage restrictions, no hidden fees.
  • 🚀 No Registration Required: Start editing instantly, protecting your privacy.
  • 🔒 Local & Secure: All data is processed locally, safe and reliable.
  • 🤖 AI-Powered: Deeply integrated with cutting-edge AI models.
  • 🎨 Feature-Rich: A professional-grade video editing experience.
