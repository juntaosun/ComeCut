什么是 ComeCut?

ComeCut is a free, AI-drivencross-platform video editing tool. Our vision is to fully leverage the power of the open-source community to build a truly free, open, and extensible AI video editing ecosystem that benefits everyone.

  • Available on Windows, macOS, Linux & Web
  • Local processing — no cloud uploads, privacy first
  • Plugin-based architecture for infinite extensibility

🤖 100+ AI Models at Your Fingertips

Seamlessly integrate the world’s top AI models to empower your video creation.

  • Supports Gemini, DeepSeek, Qwen, ComfyUI & more
  • AI subtitle translation with SRT/VTT/LRC support
  • AI speech recognition for auto-generated captions
  • The workflow supports LTX2.3, Wan2.2, Z-Image

🎬 Wide container & codec support

ComeCut supports many commonly used container formats and a wide variety of video and audio codecs. And all of them bidirectionally, too!

  • ISOBMFF-based formats (.mp4, .m4v, .m4a, ...)
  • WebM (.webm) , Ogg (.ogg) , MP3 (.mp3)
  • WAVE (.wav) , FLAC (.flac) , QuickTime File Format (.mov)
